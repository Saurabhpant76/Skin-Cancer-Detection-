Project Purpose

This AI-powered system analyzes skin lesion images and predicts possible skin diseases using Deep Learning technology.

The goal of this project is to assist in the early detection of skin cancer using computer vision and artificial intelligence.

How It Works

The model uses TensorFlow.js to process uploaded images directly inside your browser.

Your images never leave your device, improving privacy and security.

Supported Lesion Types

nv — Melanocytic Nevi

mel — Melanoma

bcc — Basal Cell Carcinoma

akiec — Actinic Keratoses

bkl — Benign Keratosis

vasc — Vascular Lesions

df — Dermatofibroma

Limitations

This project is currently a prototype and should not replace professional medical diagnosis.

Prediction accuracy may vary depending on image quality, lighting conditions, and camera quality.

Dataset & Research

The AI model was trained using the HAM10000 dataset.
